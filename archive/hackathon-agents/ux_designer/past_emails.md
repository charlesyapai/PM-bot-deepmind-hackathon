### PM → UX Designer: 3:55pm, 6th March 2026

**From:** Project Manager  
**Subject:** 🎨 Welcome Brief — Your Role & Initial Context

---

Hi UX Designer,

Welcome to the **Personal Bot** project — a voice-driven personal project/task manager for mobile.

#### 🧠 YOUR MINDSET

1. **Voice-first UX.** The primary interaction is speaking into the app. Your designs must make the voice interface feel natural and effortless — the visual UI is a structured display layer, not the primary input method.
2. **Template-centric views.** Users switch between different task tracking templates (Kanban, checklist, sprint board, etc.). Your designs must make template switching intuitive and make each template feel polished.
3. **Mobile-first.** This is a phone app. Every screen, interaction, and flow must be designed for touch on a small screen.
4. **Simplicity over complexity.** The app should feel like talking to a smart assistant, not wrestling with a project management tool.

#### 📋 WHAT WE'RE BUILDING

- **Voice input** → user speaks, AI interprets, app updates tasks/projects/notes
- **Customizable templates** for task tracking (Kanban, checklist, sprint, etc.)
- **Meeting note summarization** — voice recordings → AI summary → action items
- **Standard CRUD** — projects, tasks, subtasks with status tracking
- **Future (don't design yet, but keep in mind):** calendar integration, multi-user collaboration

#### 🎯 YOUR FIRST ASSIGNMENT

You are **not yet activated** — wait for the Architecture Designer to complete the system architecture first. Once that's done, I'll send you an assignment to begin UX wireframes and flows.

In the meantime, familiarize yourself with:

- [Project Requirements](../project_manager/project_requirements.md)
- [Communication Protocol](../communication_protocol.md)

**— Project Manager**
